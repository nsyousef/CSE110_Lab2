Finish Overview of Lego Competition

Warning: failure to meet these deadlines will result in disqualification.

The important dates for the Lego competition are as follows.

  • 4/20/2023: Deadline to submit idea for project
  • 4/20/2023 - 5/31/2023: Time to build your project
  • 6/1/2023 - 6/2/2023: Lego competition dates
  • 6/2/2023: Award ceremony

We looked at some images of some of the Lego builds that won the competition last year for inspiration.

List of Ideas for the Competition

    • would be built at minifigure scale (i.e. minifigures would be size of normal people)

    • top floor slides down when driving and slides up when parked
